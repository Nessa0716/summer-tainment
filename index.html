My Trip To Punta Cana, Dominican Republic

I stayed at the Onyx Hotel for 7 days for my sweet 16. Me and my family immediately got lunch at their buffet. There was so much to eat. Later throughout the day we constantly ran from the beach to the pools. n some of the pools were fun acitivites like Yoga on floaties, to pool volleyball. The people there are very polite and made my experience delightful. The hotel rooms were one of my favorite parts. There is a hot tub on the balcony and teh bathroom was beautiful. The whoel room had electronic low lights which were very convenient. They had lost of accomidations for room service and are always on time. The out of buffet resturants in the resort were all amazing. There were so many different types of food like mexican, greek, chinese, american, italian, and so forth. Overall the experience was a 10 out of 10 and it made my summer so much fun.
